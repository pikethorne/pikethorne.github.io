Deliverables

◇ For traditional works and digital paintings, deliverables from Pikethorne will include the following, in order, where applicable: Initial Sketch, Cleanup, Inking, Finished Product.
◇ For pixel art, deliverables will include the following: Initial Sketch, Color Blocking, Finished Product.
◇ The purpose of deliverables is to provide the context/direction for the succeeding deliverable. The commissioner is able to request 3 revisions to the initial sketch and cleanup deliverables.
◇ The commissioner understands that once the final sketch and cleanup are approved, the final piece will be created using the sketch as a foundation. The commissioner may not make any requests that would contradict what is depicted in the initial sketch.
